Education

Editing and Publishing

Editing and Publishing is a unique major which frames writing and editing as professional applications of linguistics, and it has helped me approach writing and editing with clarity, accuracy, and attention to detail. It also provided me with training on technical editing and a variety of genres of professional writing.

Linguistics Computing

Linguistics Computing is a minor focused on performing linguistic analysis with computational tools and techniques. Relevant skills I've learned in my core computational linguistics classes include web scraping, corpus compilation and analysis, sentiment analysis, syntax analysis, etc. (All in Python).

Digital Humanities and Technology

Digital Humanities and Technology is a minor primarily comprising web development and design classes. Relevant skills I've learned in this minor are the Adobe Suite (especially InDesign); front-end web development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript; and database-driven websites with MySQL and PHP.
